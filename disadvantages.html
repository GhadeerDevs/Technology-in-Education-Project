Dis-advantages of Technology in Education

While technology is beneficial, it also brings some challenges that need to be addressed:

Challenge Description / Effect Impact Level
Distraction Social media and games can divert student attention from lessons. High
Technical Issues Internet failure or software bugs can disrupt the entire learning process. Medium
Health Concerns Extended screen time can lead to eye strain and poor physical posture. High
Cost of Devices Quality hardware and software can be expensive for some families or schools. Medium
